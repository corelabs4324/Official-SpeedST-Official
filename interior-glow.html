Questions

What's included?

Two footwell light bars, two ambient dash strips, USB-C controller with remote, and a 12V accessory adapter. Everything you need for front-cabin glow.

Does it drain the battery?

The kits draw a few watts. Run them off a USB port that powers down with the car, or the supplied 12V accessory adapter.

Can I run it while driving?

Interior ambient lighting is generally fine, but steady non-distracting modes are recommended. Flashing modes are for parked use.

Will the strips damage the trim?

Everything mounts with 3M VHB and removable cable clips — no screws, no permanent marks.
